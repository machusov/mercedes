concept

Electromobility in harmony with man and nature. Eco-friendly organic battery technology and the sophisticated operating strategy of the VISION AVTR concept car.

Premier

At CES 2020, a new concept Mercedes-Benz was presented - VISION AVTR. Futuristic The concept of the presented electric car unites man, nature and the car in a unique way.

Ola Källenius, Chairman of the Board of Management of Daimler AG and Mercedes-Benz AG, presented in his speech concept car with Academy Award winner and Avatar creator James Cameron.

It was the philosophy created by the cult director in the science fiction film that became the basis for the creation of VISION AVTR.

“Mercedes-Benz has always been one of the most technologically advanced premium brands. Now it's time to unite luxury and environmental friendliness. After all, this is the only way we can meet the standards of the future.”

INTERIOR & EXTERIOR

VISION AVTR design – the epitome of environmental friendliness

The innovative VISION AVTR concept combines environmentally friendly interaction interior, exterior and UX design. The entire design process was focused on a specific result - a unique experience of interaction and perception of the concept car by the driver and passengers. It was about creating a unique space in which passengers have a biometric connection with each other, with the transport means and the surrounding world.

The side outer hole passes through the inner part and creates an endless a loop inspired by the sacred connection between the Na'vi people in the film Avatar and their natural environment a habitat.

Also, thanks to the elongated "One Bow" design and organic design philosophy, The VISION AVTR represents a radically new stage in automotive technology. Thus, having gone from the concept awareness to the unique fusion of man and car, Mercedes-Benz engineers managed to create concept electric car VISION AVTR.

A laconic continuation of the external minimalist design are the front seats, made in a very organic form that resembles leafy hammocks from the planet Pandora. The center console symbolizes The Tree of Souls, the most sacred place of the Na'vi. The control unit is intuitive and incredibly functional.

In addition, the control center is located in such a way that it is easily accessible for both driver and passenger, as well as for rear passengers.

Thus, VISION AVTR establishes a close biometric connection with the driver and Helps increase awareness of the surrounding situation.

The back of the seats and the interior of the roof are made of special fabric, which changes color. Depending on the lighting, textiles can be dark blue or delicate light blue colors.

But besides this, the environmental friendliness of the electric car interior is increased with the help of vegetarian DINAMICA® leather – the first and only microfiber that guarantees environmental performance for throughout the entire production cycle.

DINAMICA® is recycled material from old clothes, fabrics and plastics PET bottles.
